Hi, I'm Shuang Wu

a Ph.D. candidate in Management at UC San Diego and a social and organizational psychologist.

About Me I study how people navigate social hierarchy (differences in power, status, and socioeconomic status) at work. To address my research questions, I use a wide range of methodologies, including lab experiments, field surveys, archival data analysis, psychometrics, natural language processing, and qualitative coding.
